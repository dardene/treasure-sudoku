📖 THE STORY
🗺️
AN ANCIENT MAP
Deep in a forgotten sea, you stumble upon a torn, ancient map.

It speaks of Solara — a lost city of wonder, swallowed by time and hidden from the world for centuries.
🏛️
THE LOST CITY OF SOLARA
Before Solara vanished, its people hid their most sacred relics across nine sealed chambers to protect them from invaders.

The chambers were locked with ancient puzzle seals that only a true explorer could break.
⚔️
YOUR MISSION
You are that explorer.

Solve the relic puzzles carved into each chamber door. Collect the lost relics. Piece together the map.

Find the Final Vault of Solara — and claim the treasure within.

Today's Puzzle
A relic-hunting twist on classic Sudoku
  • 🎯Fill the grid — Every row, column & 3×3 box must contain all 9 relics exactly once. No repeats allowed anywhere!
  • 🃏Play from your hand — You hold 5 relics at a time. Tap one to select it, then tap an empty cell to place it. Hit Draw New when you're stuck.
  • 🔍Use the clues — Pre-filled cells are your hints. If a relic already exists in a row, column, or box, it can't go there again.
  • 💀Guard your 5 lives — Each wrong placement costs one life. Lose all 5 and the quest fails — tap Hint if you're unsure!
  • ⚡Challenges appear mid-game — Treasure bags give bonus points for quick placements. Bombs destroy relics if you don't defuse them by filling in the correct relic in time!
  • ⏱️Race for glory — Your timer starts the moment you tap Begin. Finish fast with few mistakes for the highest score!
